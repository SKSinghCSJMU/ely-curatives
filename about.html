ABOUT THE ENTERPRISE

Corporate Profile

ELY CURATIVES PRIVATE LIMITED

ELY Curatives Private Limited is a certified private limited enterprise incorporated on May 27, 2024. Our registered office and principal warehousing unit are strategically established in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.

We operate under National Industry Classification (NIC) Codes 46497 (Wholesale of pharmaceutical and medical goods) and 46491 (Wholesale of toiletries, perfumery, and cosmetics). This dual capability allows us to distribute high-barrier therapeutic tablet formulations, capsules, sugar-free wellness syrups, and antiseptic skin ointments safely.

"Upholding supreme pharmacological ethics under qualified B.Pharma supervision."

Through our professional PCD (Propaganda Cum Distribution) model, we support franchise channel partners across unexplored geographies by providing absolute monopoly rights, promotional materials, certified formulations, and stable shipping schedules.
